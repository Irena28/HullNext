Located as the next town over from Hingham and Cohasset, Hull offers the perfect blend of convenience and coastal charm. Commuters appreciate the easy 20-minute ferry ride to Boston’s Long Wharf and Logan International Airport. Boaters have convenient access to moorings and the neighboring Boston Harbor Islands. Beach lovers are drawn to Nantasket Beach in unpretentious and quirky Hull, Massachusetts, with its sandy shores, ocean views, and laid-back coastal town vibe, truly a perfect place to raise a family.
